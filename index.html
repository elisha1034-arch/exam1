Teaching Philosophy

I believe students learn best when instruction is calm, structured, and personalized to the way they think. My sessions focus on clarity and strategy, not just memorizing steps. I teach students the “why” behind problems so they can apply skills independently in class and on exams.

  • Real AP classroom experience and grading insight
  • Skill-building that creates independence, not dependency
  • Each session ends with a clear, actionable takeaway
  • Strategies: modeling, retrieval, guided practice, checklists
